Budgeting Website - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: BudgetBuddy

Reason: This name emphasizes a friendly approach to managing finances, acting as a companion for users to track their income, bills, and savings effectively.

Optional Domain: budgetbuddy.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help users effectively manage their finances by tracking income, expenses, and savings goals. It will provide tools such as a budgeting calculator, bill tracker, and income-to-expense visualizations.

Scenarios

Example User Questions:

  • How can I create a monthly budget based on my income?
  • Where can I see a breakdown of my spending categories?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #f8efef (Soft Pink) – creates a gentle and friendly aesthetic for headings and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #63453a (Rich Brown) – provides a warm, grounding contrast for backgrounds or important elements.

Typography

  • Headings: Arial, sans-serif.
  • Body Text: Roboto, sans-serif.
